Basira

Satellite-based change monitoring for Saudi Arabia. Built on 76 months of Sentinel-2 imagery over Riyadh.

01
76-Month NDVI Classification
Every pixel classified by temporal behavior across 6 years of monthly imagery. Five distinct land-cover evolution patterns identified via K-means clustering.
02
Active Hotspot Detection
Composite scoring combines vegetation loss density, surface change percentage, and NDVI anomaly overlap to surface the 10 most dynamic cells in Riyadh.
03
Change Onset Attribution
Per-cell time series pinpoint when change began. April 2022 emerges as a regional inflection point across 6 of the top 10 hotspots.
