Key Findings

🏗️ King Salman Park

Detected as the #1 largest "increased backscatter" cluster (33.5 ha). Confirmed via Google Maps as the Royal Arts Complex construction site — one of Riyadh's flagship Vision 2030 projects.

🏘️ Northern Expansion

Large decrease clusters (496 ha, 382 ha) in north Riyadh correspond to massive land clearing and grading for new suburban developments — consistent with the city's northward growth trajectory.

🌿 Vegetation Along Wadi Hanifa

Optical change detection captured vegetation increase along Wadi Hanifa — reflecting Riyadh's ongoing greening and environmental rehabilitation initiatives.

⚡ Infrastructure

Smaller high-backscatter clusters correspond to new substations and utility infrastructure. Metal structures produce strong SAR returns, making infrastructure detectable even at 10m resolution.

Market Context

Neo Space Group / UP42

PIF-owned national space company launched Saudi's first EO marketplace (sa.up42.com) in Aug 2025. 80+ data providers, serving government and enterprise. This is the ecosystem we build into.

SARsatX

Saudi SAR and optical EO company focused on Middle East and Africa. Transforms complex satellite data into actionable intelligence for security, energy, agriculture, maritime, and infrastructure.

Vision 2030 Mandate

SAR 1.285 trillion in 2025 spending on development programs (NEOM, Red Sea, Qiddiya). Massive demand for geospatial monitoring of construction progress and urban expansion.

Data Regulation

Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (Sep 2024) requires local data processing. Cross-border transfers need SDAIA contracts. Advantage for locally-built platforms.
